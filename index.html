Pranav Thakkar

Hi! I am a second-year PhD student at Cornell University, where I am advised by Prof. Tapomayukh Bhattacharjee in the EmPRISE Lab. My research focuses on using robots to dress people with mobility limitations, with everyday clothing. I am interested in haptic perception, deformable object manipulation, and safe human-robot interaction.

Previously, I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay where I worked with Prof. Leena Vachhani and Prof. Hemendra Arya. At IIT Bombay, I worked on robust state estimation techniques for ground robots with access to only bearing measurements.

I was also a research engineer at Honda R&D in Wako, Japan, where I worked on robust robot perception and mapping.
